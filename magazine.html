Manual Photography Magazine

My "Manual Photography Magazine" is a project created in semester one of year two. We as the students got a different design styles to work with to create our own magazine on anything we wanted. The style I had to work with is Constructivism. This style is heavly based on lines. As I really liked how constrictivism designs had a slant on their pages I decided to go for it as I believe is unique and eye catchy. This magazine is created to help photography enthusiasts start using manual mode on their DSLR's.
