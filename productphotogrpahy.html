Product Photography

This project is based on product photography. As we could use any object as our product I decided to go for playing cards as I was a card collector and magician enthusiast. As we had to take those pictures at home with DIY setups due to Covid-19 I found it quite challenging. As for my light source I used a desk lamp with a very cold tone bulb as my main light and a phone flash as a second light. Taking those pictures I wanted to get every unigue detail in it. As props I used American dollars, a book well known within the magic community and dice.
