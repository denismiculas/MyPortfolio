CDM Leaflet

CDM leaflet is a project we done in year one of our course. This project is based on a leaftlet for our Creative Digital Media course. We were given the content and images. We had to use the given content and images to create a leaflet. As you see I used sqares throughout my layout as I believe it enhanced the design aspect of this project. In terms of the design I got inspired from prevous leaflets created by TUD. As I wanted my design to feel as it was made by TUD I decided to follow their previous work.
