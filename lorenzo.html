Lorenzo Short Film

This porject was done by a group of four individals. Lorenzo is a project we have done semester two of year two. The goal of this project was to show positive disruption within a short film based on a boxer. The story is about Lorenzo, a boxer who wasn't doing too well before Covid-19 lockdown. He saw the lockdown as an opportunity to improve rather than a disadvantage. He worked hard to get himself ready for a competition that his coach said he wasn't ready for. Link to Lorenzo Short Film
